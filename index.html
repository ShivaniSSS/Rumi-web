Centuries have gone by, but the essence of his words is still

fresh and colorful!

Logic will bargain through twists and bends
Desire is careless of where it all ends!
When God has made the Earth for you to roam
Why have you made a prison cell your home?
The bough is cleansed as the storm sweeps,
The candle brightens as its wax sweeps.
The wound is the place where the light enters you.
Reason is powerless in the expression of love.
You have to keep breaking your heart until it opens.
I am not the hair you see, I am not the skin you see, I am the soul that lives within.
